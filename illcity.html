National Lipstick Month

Shopify Promotional Campaign designed to increase product awareness, encourage purchases, and improve the shopping experience during the National Lipstick Month sale.

Project Overview

The client launched a National Lipstick Month campaign featuring special promotions for Lip Butter products. My responsibility was to implement the campaign within Shopify by creating promotional elements across the website while ensuring a smooth customer experience.

Business Goal

Increase Sales

Encourage customers to purchase Lip Butter products through a limited time promotion.

Improve Visibility

Highlight promotional products throughout the website.

Better User Experience

Make the promotion easy to understand and accessible across devices.

My Solution

  • Designed promotional banner
  • Added Buy 1 Get 1 badge
  • Customized Shopify theme
  • Updated homepage promotional sections
  • Optimized mobile responsiveness
  • Tested implementation before deployment
